WHAT IS THE WRC?

Formed in 1973 by the FIA, the World Rally Championship is the ultimate real-world durability gauntlet for global automakers.

Unlike track racing, drivers race individually against a precise clock on rugged mountain paths, deep forest roads, and slick ice channels. It represents the purest relationship between mechanical engineering development and driver reaction skill.

ENGINEERING BENCHMARKS

Sustainable Systems: Running entirely on advanced 100% hydrocarbon fossil-free fuel matrices.

Direct Innovation: Elements perfected under intense stage stresses transition straight into everyday highway hatchbacks.
